📖 How to Play

Goal: Be the first to reach 2× (number of players) points.

Setup: Each player holds 5 cards. 3 shared cards form the Carcass.

On your turn you may:

  • Select 2+ hand cards totalling exactly 21 → click Claim 21!
  • Swap any cards between your hand and the Carcass (unlimited per turn).
  • Replace Hand — discard all 5, draw 5 fresh (first action only, only if you can't make 21).
  • Click End Turn & Draw to draw back up to 5 cards and pass.

Card values: A = 1 or 11  |  J/Q/K = 10  |  2–10 = face value

Deck empty: Pick any card from the Carcass to draw instead.

Stalemate: If nobody claims 21 through a full cycle after the deck is gone, round ends.

Scoring: Player with most sets gets n−1 pts, next n−2, etc. Full tie = nobody scores, restart.

First turn: Each player picks a face-down card — highest unique value goes first.

Keyboard shortcuts:
